<p><ul><li>On-Off Controllers</li><li>Autotuned PID Controllers</li><li>Multiloop Controllers</li><li>Safety Limit Controllers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pack temperature controllers market includes various types such as On-Off Controllers, Autotuned PID Controllers, Multiloop Controllers, Safety Limit Controllers, and others. On-Off controllers operate by turning the output fully on or fully off based on the temperature setpoint. Autotuned PID controllers use proportional, integral, and derivative control for precise temperature regulation. Multiloop controllers regulate multiple temperature zones simultaneously. Safety limit controllers ensure temperature stays within safe limits. Other types may include ramp/soak controllers, fuzzy logic controllers, and programmable logic controllers.</p></p>
