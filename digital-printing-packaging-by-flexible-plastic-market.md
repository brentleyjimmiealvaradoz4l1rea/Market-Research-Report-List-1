<p><ul><li>Electrophotography</li><li>Inkjet printing</li><li>Thermal transfer printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital printing packaging by flexible plastic market types include electrophotography, inkjet printing, and thermal transfer printing. Electrophotography involves using electrostatic charges to transfer toner onto the packaging material. Inkjet printing utilizes liquid ink droplets sprayed onto the surface of the packaging. Thermal transfer printing uses heat to transfer ink from a ribbon onto the packaging. Each market type offers unique benefits and applications for flexible plastic packaging, providing options for companies looking to customize their packaging solutions.</p></p>
