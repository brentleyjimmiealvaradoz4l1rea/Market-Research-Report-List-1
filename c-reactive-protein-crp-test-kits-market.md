<p><p>C-Reactive Protein (CRP) Test Kits Market Types include Immunoturbidimetric, ELISA, CLIA, and Other market. Immunoturbidimetric kits measure the amount of CRP in a sample by detecting changes in turbidity. ELISA kits use enzyme-linked antibodies to detect CRP levels. CLIA kits use chemiluminescent reactions to measure CRP. Other market types may include rapid CRP tests or point-of-care tests using different methods to detect CRP levels. Each type of test kit offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and ease of use.</p></p>
