<p><p>Tank linings market is segmented by product type into epoxy novolac, vinyl ester, and epoxy phenolics. Epoxy novolac offers high heat and chemical resistance, vinyl ester provides good adhesion and flexibility, while epoxy phenolics offer superior resistance to abrasion and chemicals. The market is also classified by component into one-component and two-component. One-component tank linings are ready to use, while two-component linings require mixing before application. These different types cater to the varying needs of industries requiring protective coatings for storage tanks.</p></p>
