<p><p>Ultra-Violet Fluorescent Ink comes in two main types - Invisible UV Fluorescent Ink and Visible UV Fluorescent Ink. Invisible UV Fluorescent Ink is used for security and discreet marking purposes, as it is only visible under ultraviolet light. On the other hand, Visible UV Fluorescent Ink is visible under normal lighting conditions and is commonly used in printing, packaging, and decorative applications. Both types of UV Fluorescent Ink offer unique properties and are utilized in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
