<p><p>Rugby gloves are categorized into two main types based on finger coverage: Half Finger and Full Finger. Half Finger gloves provide protection for the palm and lower part of the fingers, leaving the top part exposed. These gloves offer better grip and flexibility for handling the ball. On the other hand, Full Finger gloves cover the entire finger length, providing maximum protection against impacts and abrasions. They are preferred by players seeking extra support and warmth during cold weather conditions. Both types cater to different player preferences and playing styles.</p></p>
