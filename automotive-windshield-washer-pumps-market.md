<p><p>Automotive windshield washer pumps serve the purpose of spraying windshield washer fluid onto the windshield to clean it efficiently. There are two main types of pumps commonly used in the market: mono pump and dual pump. Mono pumps consist of a single pump that supplies fluid to both the front and rear windshield wipers. On the other hand, dual pumps have separate pumps for the front and rear windshield wipers, allowing for more precise control and distribution of washer fluid.</p></p>
