<p><ul><li>Biochemical</li><li>Thermochemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-refinery plants can be classified into two main market types: biochemical and thermochemical. Biochemical bio-refinery plants utilize biological processes such as fermentation or enzymatic reactions to convert biomass into valuable products like biofuels, biochemicals, and bioplastics. Thermochemical bio-refinery plants, on the other hand, employ heat and chemical reactions to break down biomass into products like syngas, bio-oil, and biochar. Both market types play a crucial role in the transition towards a more sustainable and circular economy by efficiently utilizing biomass resources.</p></p>
