<p><strong>The Industrial Wire Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intumescent Wire Coatings</li><li>Non-Intumescent Wire Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial wire coatings market includes two main types: intumescent wire coatings and non-intumescent wire coatings. Intumescent coatings are designed to expand when exposed to high temperatures, creating a protective barrier that insulates the wire and prevents fire spread. Non-intumescent coatings, on the other hand, provide a durable and corrosion-resistant layer to the wire without expanding when heated. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in enhancing the safety and longevity of industrial wires in various applications.</p></p>
