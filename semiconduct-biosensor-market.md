<p><p>Semiconductor biosensors are categorized by the type of biological receptor they use to detect target analytes. Enzyme-based biosensors utilize enzymes to catalyze a reaction and produce an electrical signal. Nucleic acid biosensors detect specific DNA or RNA sequences. Lectin biosensors recognize and bind to carbohydrate molecules. Antibody biosensors use specific antibodies to bind to target antigens. Cell-based biosensors utilize living cells to detect changes in their environment. Organ-based biosensors use tissues or organs to measure biological responses.</p></p>
