<p><strong>The Sodium PCA Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L-form</li><li>DL-form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sodium PCA is available in two forms in the market: L-form and DL-form. The L-form is the natural, biologically active form of Sodium PCA, which is more commonly used in skincare products due to its superior hydrating and moisturizing properties. On the other hand, the DL-form is a racemic mixture of both L and D forms, which is less stable and less effective compared to the L-form. When choosing Sodium PCA products, it is recommended to opt for those containing the L-form for optimal results.</p></p>
