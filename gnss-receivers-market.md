<p><strong>The GNSS Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Frequency GNSS Receiver</li><li>Multi-GNSS Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GNSS receivers are devices used for tracking and receiving signals from Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS) like GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou. Single-frequency GNSS receivers receive signals from a single GNSS system, usually GPS. They provide basic positioning accuracy and are cost-effective. On the other hand, multi-GNSS receivers can receive signals from multiple satellite systems simultaneously, providing more accurate and reliable positioning information. They offer enhanced performance and are capable of operating in challenging environments where single-frequency receivers may struggle.</p></p>
