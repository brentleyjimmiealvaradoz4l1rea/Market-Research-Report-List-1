<p><p>Automotive Rigid PCB market consists of Single Layer Rigid PCB, which are suitable for applications where simple circuits are required. Double Layer Rigid PCBs, on the other hand, have two layers of copper with a substrate in between, providing more space for complex circuits. Multilayer Rigid PCBs have more than two layers, allowing for even more complex circuit designs and higher component density. Each type caters to different automotive applications based on the complexity of the circuit required.</p></p>
