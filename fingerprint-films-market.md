<p><strong>The Fingerprint Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Fingerprint Film</li><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fingerprint films use light reflection to capture an image of the fingerprint, while capacitive fingerprint films detect the electrical charge differences in the skin's ridges and valleys. While both types of fingerprint films are used for biometric authentication, capacitive films are generally more reliable and secure for high-security applications. Optical films are typically more affordable and easier to integrate into different devices. Ultimately, the choice between the two types of fingerprint films depends on the specific needs and requirements of the application.</p></p>
