<p><p>Direct-to-oven trays are available in three main types: metal, corrugated, and plastic. Metal trays are durable and offer even heat distribution, making them ideal for roasting and baking. Corrugated trays are disposable, cost-effective, and lightweight, making them suitable for single-use or large-scale cooking. Plastic trays are convenient and can be used for both cooking and serving, but may not offer the same heat retention as metal trays. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific cooking needs and preferences.</p></p>
