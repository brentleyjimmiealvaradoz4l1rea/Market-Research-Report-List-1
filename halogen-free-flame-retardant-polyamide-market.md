<p><p>Halogen-free flame retardant polyamide market includes types such as red phosphorus, melamine salts, and others. Red phosphorus is an effective flame retardant as it releases phosphoric acid when heated, forming a protective layer. Melamine salts work by releasing nitrogen when exposed to high temperatures, which dilutes the flammable gases. Other types of flame retardants in this market may include additives such as aluminum hydroxide or magnesium hydroxide, which work by releasing water vapor to reduce the temperature of the flame.</p></p>
