<p><p>Micro video lenses market is divided into three main types: Standard Lenses, Fisheye Lenses, and Wide Angle Lenses. Standard Lenses provide a basic view with no distortion, suitable for everyday shooting. Fisheye Lenses offer a wide field of view with a distorted, rounded appearance, perfect for artistic and creative shots. Wide Angle Lenses provide a wider perspective than standard lenses, making them ideal for capturing landscapes and tight spaces. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the world of micro video photography.</p></p>
