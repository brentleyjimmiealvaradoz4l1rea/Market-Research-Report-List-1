<p><p>The vacuum pump brake market can be divided into two main types: mechanical type and electric type. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications. The choice between mechanical and electric vacuum pump brakes depends on factors such as performance requirements, cost, and ease of installation.</p></p>
