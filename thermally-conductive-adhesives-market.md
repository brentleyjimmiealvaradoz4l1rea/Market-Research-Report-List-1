<p><p>Thermally Conductive Adhesives can be divided into two main types: Silicon-based and Non-silicon based. Silicon-based adhesives are known for their high thermal conductivity, excellent adhesion, and stability at high temperatures. On the other hand, Non-silicon based adhesives are typically made from materials like epoxies, acrylics, or polyurethanes and offer a wider range of flexibility and compatibility with various substrates. Both types of adhesives are commonly used in electronics, automotive, aerospace, and other industries where thermal management is crucial.</p></p>
