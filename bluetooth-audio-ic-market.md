<p><strong>The Bluetooth Audio IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Bluetooth Audio Chip</li><li>Audio SoC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ordinary Bluetooth Audio Chip refers to a standalone chip that provides basic Bluetooth audio functionality, such as connecting audio devices wirelessly. On the other hand, the Audio SoC market includes Bluetooth Audio System-on-Chip, which integrates additional features and capabilities into one chip, such as audio processing, DAC (Digital-to-Analog Converter), and amplifier. This allows for more advanced audio processing and improved sound quality in Bluetooth audio devices. Both types serve the purpose of enabling wireless audio connections but cater to different levels of functionality and performance.</p></p>
