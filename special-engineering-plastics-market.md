<p><strong>The Special Engineering Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PPS</li><li>PI</li><li>PEEK</li><li>LCP</li><li>PSF</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Special engineering plastics market can be classified into various types such as PPS (polyphenylene sulfide), PI (polyimide), PEEK (polyether ether ketone), LCP (liquid crystal polymer), PSF (polysulfone), and others. PPS is known for its high dimensional stability and chemical resistance. PI offers excellent heat resistance and mechanical properties. PEEK is valued for its exceptional thermal and mechanical performance. LCP is preferred for its high strength and stiffness. PSF is chosen for its transparency and resistance to heat and chemicals.</p></p>
