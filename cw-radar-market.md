<p><ul><li>Single Frequency</li><li>Multiple Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CW radar can be classified into two main types based on frequency: Single Frequency and Multiple Frequency. Single Frequency CW radar operates at a fixed frequency and provides continuous wave signals for measurement. On the other hand, Multiple Frequency CW radar uses multiple frequencies simultaneously to provide more accurate and detailed information. Single Frequency radar is simpler and more cost-effective, while Multiple Frequency radar offers higher resolution and detection capabilities. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on the specific requirements.</p></p>
