<p><p>Natural gas pipelines can be categorized into three main types based on their function within the energy supply chain. Gathering pipelines collect natural gas from production facilities and transport it to processing plants. Transportation pipelines move the processed natural gas over long distances to distribution centers or end users. Distribution pipelines deliver the natural gas directly to homes, businesses, and other consumers. Each type plays a critical role in the efficient and reliable delivery of natural gas throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
