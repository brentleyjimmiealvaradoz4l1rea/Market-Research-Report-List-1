<p><strong>The Mass Flow Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Hot Film Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Vane Air Flow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mass flow sensors are commonly categorized into four main types - Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor, Hot Film Air Flow Sensor, Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor, and Vane Air Flow Sensor. Hot Wire Air Flow Sensors measure air flow by detecting changes in the electrical resistance of a heated wire. Hot Film Air Flow Sensors use a thin film element to measure air flow. Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensors utilize the principle of vortex shedding to measure flow. Vane Air Flow Sensors use a vane that moves with changes in air flow to measure flow rate.</p></p>
