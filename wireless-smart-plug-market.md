<p><p>Wireless smart plugs are devices that can be plugged into a regular electrical socket and enable control over the connected appliances remotely through a smartphone or voice assistant. Bluetooth smart plugs use a short-range wireless technology to connect directly to a paired device, while Wi-Fi smart plugs connect to the internet through a home network, allowing control from anywhere. Both types offer convenience in managing devices remotely, but Bluetooth smart plugs have a limited range while Wi-Fi smart plugs provide more flexibility and control options.</p></p>
