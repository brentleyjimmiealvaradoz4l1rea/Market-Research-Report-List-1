<p><p>Ultrafiltration (UF) System is a type of filtration process that uses a porous membrane to separate suspended solids, bacteria, viruses, and other contaminants from water. There are two main types of UF systems: Polymeric Ultrafiltration (UF) System and Ceramic Ultrafiltration (UF) System. Polymeric UF systems use polymer membranes, while Ceramic UF systems use ceramic membranes. Both types are effective in reducing turbidity, removing pathogens, and improving water quality, making them popular choices for various applications including water treatment, wastewater recycling, and industrial processes.</p></p>
