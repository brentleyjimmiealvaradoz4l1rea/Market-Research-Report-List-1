<p><p>Flexible electronics and circuit market includes three main types of circuits: single-sided, multilayer, and rigid. Single-sided circuits have components and wiring on only one side of the substrate, allowing for flexibility. Multilayer circuits have multiple layers of conductive materials sandwiched between insulating layers, providing higher performance and complexity. Rigid circuits, on the other hand, are typically made of inflexible materials like fiberglass and cannot be bent or twisted. Each type serves different purposes and applications in the growing flexible electronics industry.</p></p>
