<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray foam sealant market is divided into two main types: one component and two component. One component sealants are ready to use straight from the can, requiring no mixing before application. They are convenient and easy to use for smaller projects. On the other hand, two component sealants require mixing of two separate components before application, allowing for better control over the curing process. They are typically used for larger projects and provide a stronger bond and better insulation properties.</p></p>
