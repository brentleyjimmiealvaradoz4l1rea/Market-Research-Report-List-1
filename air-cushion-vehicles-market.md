<p><strong>The Air-cushion Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skirted Air-cushion Vehicles</li><li>Sidewall Air-cushion Vehicles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air-cushion vehicles (ACVs) can be categorized into two main types: Skirted ACVs and Sidewall ACVs. Skirted ACVs have a flexible skirt around their perimeter, which traps air and creates a cushion beneath the vehicle, allowing it to hover over surfaces like water or land. On the other hand, Sidewall ACVs have inflatable side walls that provide the necessary air cushion for lift and propulsion. Both types of ACVs offer unique benefits and are used in various applications such as military, transportation, and leisure.</p></p>
