<p><ul><li>Absolute Angle Measuring Encoders</li><li>Incremental Angle Measuring Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Angle Measuring Encoders are devices used to measure the rotational position of a shaft. There are two main types in the market: Absolute Angle Measuring Encoders and Incremental Angle Measuring Encoders. Absolute encoders provide a unique digital code for each position, allowing for precise and accurate measurement without needing a reference point. Incremental encoders generate a series of pulses when the shaft is rotated, requiring a reference point to determine the exact position. Both types have their own applications and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
