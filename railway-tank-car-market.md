<p><p>Railway tank car market is divided into two main types: Pressurized Tank Car and Non-pressurized Tank Car. Pressurized tank cars are designed to transport gases or liquids under high pressure, providing a controlled environment for volatile materials. Non-pressurized tank cars, on the other hand, are used for non-hazardous liquid materials and do not require high pressure storage. Both types of tank cars play a critical role in the transportation of various goods across the railway network, ensuring the safe and efficient delivery of products.</p></p>
