<p><strong>The LTE Femto Base Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time Division Duplex (TDD)</li><li>Frequency Division Duplex (FDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LTE Femto Base Station market consists of two main types: Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD). In TDD, the uplink and downlink share the same frequency band but operate at different times. This allows for flexible allocation of resources based on traffic demand. On the other hand, FDD uses separate frequency bands for uplink and downlink communications, providing simultaneous transmission in both directions. Both TDD and FDD offer unique advantages, making them suitable for different use cases in the LTE Femto Base Station market.</p></p>
