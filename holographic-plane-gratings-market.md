<p><p>Holographic plane gratings are classified into two main types: transmission grating and reflective grating. Transmission gratings are designed to diffract light as it passes through the grating material, splitting the light into its various components. Reflective gratings, on the other hand, are used to reflect light off the surface of the grating, redirecting and dispersing the light. Both types are essential components in various optical devices and systems, catering to different applications and industries.</p></p>
