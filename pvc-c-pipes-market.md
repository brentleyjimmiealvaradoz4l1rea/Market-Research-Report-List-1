<p><p>PVC-C Pipes come in two main market types: Schedule 40 and Schedule 80. Schedule 40 PVC-C pipes are lighter in weight and less expensive, making them ideal for residential plumbing and irrigation systems. On the other hand, Schedule 80 PVC-C pipes are thicker and stronger, designed for industrial applications that require higher pressure and durability. Both are commonly used for water distribution, sewer systems, and chemical processing, but the choice between the two depends on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
