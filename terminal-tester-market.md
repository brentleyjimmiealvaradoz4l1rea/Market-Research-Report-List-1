<p><p>Terminal testers are tools used to check the electrical connections in terminals. There are two types of terminal testers based on the number of probes they have - single hole and double hole. Single hole testers have one probe for checking the connection in a terminal, while double hole testers have two probes for more comprehensive testing. Single hole testers are simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for basic troubleshooting. Double hole testers offer more precise testing, making them ideal for complex electrical systems and advanced diagnostics.</p></p>
