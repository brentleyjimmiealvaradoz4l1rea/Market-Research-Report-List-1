<p><p>Thermoplastic hose market offers various types of hoses such as twin hoses and multiple hoses. Twin hoses consist of two parallel hoses within a single outer cover, designed for simultaneous fluid transfer. Multiple hoses, on the other hand, involve more than two hoses bundled together for complex applications requiring multiple fluid transfers. These hose types cater to a wide range of industries including automotive, aerospace, and industrial manufacturing, providing flexibility and durability for different fluid transfer needs.</p></p>
