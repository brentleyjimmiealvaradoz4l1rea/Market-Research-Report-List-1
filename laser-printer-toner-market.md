<p><strong>The Laser Printer Toner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic</li><li>Non-Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser printer toner can be categorized into two main types based on their composition: magnetic and non-magnetic toner. Magnetic toner consists of iron oxide particles that are attracted by the magnetic components of the printer drum, allowing for precise transfer onto the paper. Non-magnetic toner, on the other hand, does not contain magnetic properties and relies on electrical charges for transferring onto the paper. Both types of toner have their own advantages and are used in various types of laser printers.</p></p>
