<p><p>Pressure-sensitive graphic films are a type of self-adhesive material used for creating graphics on surfaces. The market is divided into four main types: cast vinyl films, calendered vinyl films, engineered films, and other films. Cast vinyl films are highly conformable and durable, making them ideal for complex surfaces. Calendered vinyl films are more affordable and easier to install, but less durable. Engineered films are specialty films designed for specific applications. Other films include reflective films, metallic films, and textured films.</p></p>
