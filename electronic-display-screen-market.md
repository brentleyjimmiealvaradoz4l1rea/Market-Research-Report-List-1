<p><p>The electronic display screen market consists of three main types - LCD (Liquid Crystal Display), LED (Light Emitting Diode), and OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode). LCD screens use liquid crystal technology to display images, LED screens use light emitting diodes for backlighting, and OLED screens use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied. Each type of display screen offers unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
