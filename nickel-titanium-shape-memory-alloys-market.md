<p><p>Nickel-Titanium Shape Memory Alloys Market is divided into two main types based on temperature phases: High Temperature Austenite Phase and Low Temperature Martensite Phase. In the High Temperature Austenite Phase, the alloy retains its original shape, while in the Low Temperature Martensite Phase, the alloy can be deformed and will return to its original shape when heated. These market types cater to different applications and industries requiring shape memory properties at different temperature ranges.</p></p>
