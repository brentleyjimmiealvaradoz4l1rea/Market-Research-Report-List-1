<p><strong>The Window Hardware Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Handle</li><li>Connecting Piece</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Window hardware market can be classified into three main types: handle, connecting piece, and others. Handles include knobs, levers, and cranks that are used to open and close windows. Connecting pieces such as hinges, glides, and locks are important for the functionality and security of windows. The "others" category is made up of various accessories like weather-stripping, seals, and screens that enhance the performance and appearance of windows. Overall, these different types of window hardware play a crucial role in ensuring the proper operation and maintenance of windows.</p></p>
