<p><p>Baby bouncers come in two main types - electric and manual. Electric baby bouncers are powered by batteries or electricity and provide continuous bouncing motions for babies. They are typically more affordable and do not require any power source, making them a convenient option for parents on the go. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of parents.</p></p>
