<p><p>Vehicle Mounted LiDAR is a technology used for creating high-resolution 3D maps of surrounding environments. There are two main types of Vehicle Mounted LiDAR systems: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes solid-state components such as semiconductors to emit and receive laser pulses, offering a compact and robust solution. On the other hand, Mechanical LiDAR relies on moving parts like rotating mirrors to scan the surroundings. Both types have their unique advantages and limitations, catering to different applications and budget constraints in the market.</p></p>
