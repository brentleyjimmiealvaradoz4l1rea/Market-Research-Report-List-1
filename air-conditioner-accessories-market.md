<p><p>Air conditioner accessories market includes various types of components essential for the proper functioning of an AC unit. Refrigerant, Compressor, Condenser Coil, Expansion Valve, and Evaporator Coil are some key components in this market. Refrigerant is responsible for cooling and dehumidifying the air, while the compressor pressurizes the refrigerant. The condenser coil releases heat outside, the expansion valve regulates the flow of refrigerant, and the evaporator coil absorbs heat from the indoor air. These components work together to ensure efficient cooling performance in air conditioning systems.</p></p>
