<p><p>DSL modem routers come in different market types, such as 802.11ac and 802.11b/g/n. Both market types cater to different consumer needs, with 802.11ac being more advanced and suitable for heavy internet usage, while 802.11b/g/n is more budget-friendly and suitable for basic internet needs.</p></p>
