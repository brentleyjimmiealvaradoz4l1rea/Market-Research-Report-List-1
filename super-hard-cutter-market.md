<p><p>The super hard cutter market is divided into three main types: High-speed steels, hard alloys, and others. High-speed steels are known for their ability to maintain hardness and toughness at high temperatures, making them ideal for cutting applications. Hard alloys, also known as cemented carbides, are made from a combination of tungsten carbide and cobalt, providing high wear resistance and hardness. The "Others" category typically includes materials such as ceramic and cubic boron nitride, which offer unique properties for specific cutting tasks.</p></p>
