<p><p>Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) heaters come in two main types - Straight Heater and Oval Heater. A Straight Heater is a more traditional design that allows for uniform heating throughout the DEF tank. On the other hand, an Oval Heater is designed to fit specific tank shapes, such as oval or irregularly shaped tanks, ensuring efficient heating in those unique configurations. Both types of heaters are essential in ensuring DEF remains in its liquid form, preventing crystallization and ensuring proper vehicle emissions control.</p></p>
