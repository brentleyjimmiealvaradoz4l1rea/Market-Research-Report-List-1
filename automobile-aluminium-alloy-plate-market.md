<p><p>Automobile Aluminium Alloy Plate Market Types refer to the different methods of production used in manufacturing aluminum plates for automotive applications. Cold Rolling involves passing the aluminum through rollers at room temperature to achieve a smooth and polished finish. Hot Rolling, on the other hand, involves heating the aluminum to high temperatures before passing it through rollers, resulting in a thinner and stronger plate. Both methods have their advantages and are used in the automotive industry based on specific requirements and desired properties of the alloy plates.</p></p>
