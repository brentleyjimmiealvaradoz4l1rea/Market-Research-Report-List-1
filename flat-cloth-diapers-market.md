<p><p>Flat cloth diapers are available in two main market types: single-layer and multi-layer. Single-layer diapers consist of a single layer of fabric, making them lightweight, breathable, and quick-drying. They are ideal for newborns and hot climates. On the other hand, multi-layer diapers are made up of several layers of fabric, offering greater absorbency and leak protection. They are suitable for heavier wetters and nighttime use. Both types of diapers are popular choices among eco-conscious parents looking for reusable and sustainable diapering options.</p></p>
