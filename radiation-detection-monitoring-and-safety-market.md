<p><p>Radiation detection, monitoring, and safety market offers various types of devices to detect and measure radiation levels, ensuring safety in different industries. Geiger counter, commonly used for finding radioactive contamination, detects radiation using the ionization effect. Scintillation detector employs a crystal that produces flashes of light when struck by radiation, enabling measurement. Solid-state detectors use semiconductors to detect radiation and provide accurate readings. Other devices include thermoluminescent dosimeters and personal radiation detectors, enabling precise monitoring of radiation exposure. These technologies play a crucial role in minimizing radiation hazards and protecting human health.</p></p>
