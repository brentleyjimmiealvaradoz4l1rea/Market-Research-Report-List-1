<p><strong>The Inorganic Conductive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Curing Reaction Type</li><li>Hot Melt</li><li>High Temperature Sintering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inorganic conductive adhesives are classified into three types based on curing reaction mechanism. Curing reaction type adhesives rely on chemical reactions to cure, providing strong bonds. Hot melt adhesives are applied in a molten state and solidify upon cooling, offering fast curing and convenient application. High temperature sintering adhesives require heat treatment at elevated temperatures to form a conductive bond, suitable for applications where extreme durability and conductivity are required. Each type offers unique advantages for different industrial applications.</p></p>
