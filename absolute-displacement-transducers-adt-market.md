<p><p>Absolute Displacement Transducers (ADT) market can be categorized into Linear Absolute Displacement Transducers and Angular Absolute Displacement Transducers. Linear ADTs measure linear movement along a single axis, while Angular ADTs measure rotational movement around a central point. Other types of ADTs include multi-axis transducers that can measure displacement in multiple directions simultaneously. These devices play a crucial role in various industries where precise and accurate measurement of displacement is required, such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
