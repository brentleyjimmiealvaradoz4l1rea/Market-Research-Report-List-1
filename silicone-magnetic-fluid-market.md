<p><p>Silicone Magnetic Fluid Market includes three types: ISF Type, IHF Type, and ASF Type. ISF Type is characterized by its low viscosity and excellent thermal stability, making it suitable for applications in the aerospace and automotive industries. IHF Type features a high viscosity and superior magnetic properties, making it ideal for use in electronic devices and audio equipment. ASF Type combines the characteristics of both ISF and IHF types, providing a balance of viscosity and thermal stability for various industrial applications.</p></p>
