<p><strong>The Concrete Pipe Pile Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-tensioned Concrete Pipe Pile</li><li>Post-tensioned Concrete Pipe Pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete pipe piles are used in various construction projects for foundation support. Pre-tensioned concrete pipe piles are prefabricated with steel tendons that are tensioned prior to placement, providing higher load-bearing capacity. Post-tensioned concrete pipe piles have steel tendons inserted after placement and tensioned, improving the pile's strength and stability. While pre-tensioned piles are more commonly used in smaller projects due to quicker installation, post-tensioned piles are preferred for larger projects requiring higher load capacities. Both types offer reliable foundation solutions for construction projects.</p></p>
