<p><p>Non-coating Grade Titanium Dioxide market is divided into two main types based on the production process: Sulfate Process and Chloride Process. The Sulfate Process involves treating titanium ore with sulfuric acid, resulting in a lower purity product suitable for non-coating applications. On the other hand, the Chloride Process uses chlorine to produce a higher quality and more expensive product primarily used in coatings and pigments. Each process caters to different market segments with specific requirements and price points.</p></p>
