<p><p>Manual hermetic hinged doors are available in two main market types: single leaf and double leaves. Single leaf doors consist of one door panel that swings open, providing a more compact and cost-effective solution. Double leaf doors, on the other hand, consist of two door panels that open in the middle, offering a wider opening for larger spaces. Both types of doors provide a secure and airtight seal when closed, making them ideal for environments where hygiene and air quality control are important.</p></p>
