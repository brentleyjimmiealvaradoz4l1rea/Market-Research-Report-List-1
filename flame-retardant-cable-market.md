<p><p>Flame retardant cables are classified into three main types based on their composition and performance characteristics. Low-smoke halogen-free cables are environmentally friendly and emit low levels of smoke and toxic gases when exposed to fire. Low-smoke low-halogen cables have reduced levels of halogen compounds, making them safer for human health and the environment. Low-smoke halogen cables, on the other hand, contain halogen compounds but emit low levels of smoke when burned. Each type caters to different requirements and regulations in the market.</p></p>
