<p><ul><li>Solid Fixing Agent</li><li>Liquid Fixing Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixing agents are chemicals used in the textile industry to improve the colorfastness of dyes on fabrics. There are two main types of fixing agents: solid fixing agents and liquid fixing agents. </p><p>Solid fixing agents are in powder or granular form and are commonly used in batch dyeing processes. They are easy to store and transport, but may require additional steps for dissolution.</p><p>Liquid fixing agents are in liquid form and are often preferred for continuous dyeing processes as they are easier to handle and apply. They can be directly added to the dye bath, offering convenience and efficiency.</p></p>
