Day Wear Lens are designed to be worn during the day and removed at night, providing clear vision and comfort for daily activities. Night Wear Lens, also known as orthokeratology or Ortho-K, are worn overnight to reshape the cornea, allowing for clear vision during the day without the need for glasses or contact lenses. Both types of RGP lenses offer benefits such as improved vision, durability, and oxygen permeability.</p></p>
