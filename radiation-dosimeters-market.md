<p><p>Radiation Dosimeters market includes various types such as LTDs (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters), EPDs (Electronic Personal Dosimeters), MOSFET (metal oxide semiconductor field-effect transistor) Dosimeters, and others. LTDs measure radiation exposure by detecting light emitted when heated, while EPDs are electronic devices that provide real-time radiation dose readings. MOSFET Dosimeters use semiconductor technology to measure radiation exposure. Other types of dosimeters in the market may include optically stimulated luminescence (OSL) dosimeters, film badges, and pocket ionization chambers. Each type offers unique features and benefits for monitoring radiation exposure.</p></p>
