<p><p>Porous Metal Material Market Types can be categorized into Independent Hole Type and Continuous Hole Type. Independent Hole Type porous metal materials have isolated holes that are not interconnected, allowing for specific flow control and filtration properties. On the other hand, Continuous Hole Type porous metal materials have interconnected holes that allow for more uniform flow distribution and permeability. Each type has unique characteristics and applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
