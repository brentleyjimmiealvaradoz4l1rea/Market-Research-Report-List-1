<p><ul><li>Pallet Transporting Truck</li><li>Pallet Stacking Truck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric pallet trucks can be divided into two main types: pallet transporting trucks and pallet stacking trucks. </p><p>Pallet transporting trucks are designed to efficiently move pallets from one location to another within a warehouse or facility. They are equipped with a platform to hold the pallet securely during transportation.</p><p>Pallet stacking trucks, on the other hand, are specifically designed to lift and stack pallets vertically, making them ideal for warehouses with limited space. These trucks are equipped with a mast that can raise and lower pallets as needed.</p></p>
