<p><p>Smart Fleet Management market includes various types such as Tracking, Optimization, ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems), and Remote Diagnostics. Optimization focuses on maximizing fleet operations through route planning, fuel management, and resource allocation. ADAS integrates technologies like collision avoidance and lane departure warning to enhance driver safety. Remote Diagnostics allow fleet managers to remotely monitor vehicle health and performance, enabling proactive maintenance and reducing downtimes.</p></p>
