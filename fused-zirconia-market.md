<p><p>Fused Zirconia market types include Electrically Fused Zirconia and Chemical Synthesis Zirconia. Electrically Fused Zirconia is produced by heating zircon sand to a molten state and then cooling it to create a dense, high-purity material. On the other hand, Chemical Synthesis Zirconia involves the chemical reaction between zirconium compounds to form zirconia powder, which is then processed into various products. Both methods result in high-quality zirconia materials with different properties and applications in industries such as ceramics, electronics, and aerospace.</p></p>
