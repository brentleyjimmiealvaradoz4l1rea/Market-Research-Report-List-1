<p><p>The lacrosse helmet market consists of two main types: Expanded Polypropylene (EPP) and Double Density Vinyl Nitrile (VN). EPP helmets are preferred for their lightweight and durable properties, providing good impact absorption. On the other hand, VN helmets are known for their dual-density construction that offers enhanced protection against high-impact collisions.
