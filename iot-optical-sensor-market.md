<p><p>IoT Optical Sensor market types include different categories such as photoconductive devices, photovoltaic equipment, photodiodes, phototransistors, and other specialized sensors. Photoconductive devices change their resistance when exposed to light, while photovoltaic equipment produce voltage when illuminated. Photodiodes convert light into current, and phototransistors amplify light signals. Other sensors in this market may include fiber optic sensors or infrared sensors. Each type of optical sensor plays a unique role in capturing and interpreting light signals for IoT applications.</p></p>
