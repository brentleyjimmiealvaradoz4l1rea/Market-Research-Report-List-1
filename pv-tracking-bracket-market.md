<p><p>PV Tracking Bracket Market includes Single Axis PV Tracking Bracket and Dual Axis PV Tracking Bracket. Single Axis PV Tracking Bracket market focuses on systems that move solar panels along one axis, typically following the sun's daily east to west movement. This type of bracket increases energy production by optimizing the angle of the panels throughout the day. On the other hand, Dual Axis PV Tracking Bracket market includes systems that move solar panels along two axes, both vertically and horizontally, to track the sun's movement throughout the day and year, maximizing energy output.</p></p>
