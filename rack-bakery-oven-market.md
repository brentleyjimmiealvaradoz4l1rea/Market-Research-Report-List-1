<p><p>Rack bakery ovens come in two main types: electric and steam-based. Electric rack ovens use electric heating elements to create the heat needed for baking, offering precise temperature control and even distribution of heat. On the other hand, steam-based rack ovens use a combination of steam and heat to produce high-quality baked goods with a crispy crust and moist interior. Both types of rack ovens are popular in the bakery industry for their efficiency, consistency, and ability to handle large baking batches.</p></p>
