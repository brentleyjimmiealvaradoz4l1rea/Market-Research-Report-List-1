<p><p>The smartphone holder market is divided into two main types: magnetic and fasten holders. Fasten holders, on the other hand, use clamps or clips to grip the phone, providing a more secure fit. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of the consumers, with magnetic holders offering convenience and ease of use, while fasten holders provide added security and stability.</p></p>
