<p><p>Denim materials are categorized into three main market types based on their weight: lightweight, mid-weight, and heavyweight. Lightweight denim, typically under 12 ounces, is known for its soft, comfortable feel and is popular for making lighter-weight garments. Mid-weight denim, ranging from 12 to 16 ounces, strikes a balance between durability and comfort, making it ideal for a variety of clothing items. Heavyweight denim, above 16 ounces, is renowned for its rugged durability and is commonly used in workwear and other heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
