<p><p>Transparent dressings are a type of wound dressing that are clear and allow for easy visibility of the wound. There are two main types of transparent dressings: transparent wound dressing and transparent self-adhesive dressing. Transparent wound dressings are applied directly onto the wound and provide a barrier to protect it from infection while allowing the healthcare provider to monitor the healing process. Transparent self-adhesive dressings are similar, but they have an adhesive backing that helps them stick securely to the skin and maintains a moist environment for better wound healing.</p></p>
