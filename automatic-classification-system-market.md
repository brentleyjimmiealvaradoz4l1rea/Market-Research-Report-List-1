<p><p>Automatic Classification System market consists of various types, including Linear Classification Systems and Loop Classification Systems. Linear Classification Systems use linear decision boundaries to categorize data points into distinct classes based on specific features. On the other hand, Loop Classification Systems utilize iterative processes to continuously refine classification results, adjusting parameters until an optimal solution is achieved. Both types play a crucial role in automating the process of categorizing and organizing data, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in various industries.</p></p>
