<p><p>Industrial gloves market can be categorized into disposable gloves and reusable gloves. Disposable gloves are single-use gloves made of materials like latex, nitrile, or vinyl. They are used for short-term tasks to prevent contamination and reduce the risk of exposure to chemicals or pathogens. Reusable gloves are made of durable materials like rubber or leather and can be washed and reused multiple times. They are ideal for longer-term tasks that require more protection and durability. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the industrial sector.</p></p>
