<p><ul><li>Mineral</li><li>Chemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete admixture construction chemicals are categorized into two main types: mineral admixtures and chemical admixtures. Mineral admixtures, such as fly ash, silica fume, and slag, are derived from natural sources and enhance the properties of concrete by improving workability, strength, and durability. Chemical admixtures, on the other hand, are synthetic substances added to concrete mixtures to achieve specific characteristics like accelerating or retarding the setting time, reducing water content, or enhancing workability. Both types of admixtures play a crucial role in modern construction practices.</p></p>
