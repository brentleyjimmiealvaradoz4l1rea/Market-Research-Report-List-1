Connectors with a mated height below 1 mm are ideal for applications requiring minimal space, while those in the 1-2 mm range offer a slightly larger profile. Connectors in the 2-10 mm category are versatile and commonly used in various industries. Edge card connectors with a mated height above 10 mm are typically used in applications where a higher profile is required.</p></p>
