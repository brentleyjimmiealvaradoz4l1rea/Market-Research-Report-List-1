<p><ul><li>Passive Infrared Sensor</li><li>Imaging Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared motion sensors can be divided into two main types - Passive Infrared Sensor (PIR) and Imaging Infrared Sensor. PIR sensors detect motion by measuring infrared radiation emitted by objects in their field of view. They are commonly used in security systems and lighting applications. Imaging Infrared Sensors, on the other hand, create an image of the infrared radiation emitted by objects, allowing for more detailed analysis of motion. They are often used in surveillance and military applications for advanced monitoring and tracking capabilities.</p></p>
