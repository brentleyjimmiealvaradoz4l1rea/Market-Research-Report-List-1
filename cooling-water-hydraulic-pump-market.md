<p><strong>The Cooling Water Hydraulic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston</li><li>Positive-Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cooling Water Hydraulic Pump market consists of two main types: Piston and Positive-Displacement pumps. </p><p>Piston pumps use reciprocating pistons to create pressure and move fluid through the system. These pumps are known for their high efficiency and ability to handle high pressures.</p><p>Positive-Displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of fluid and then displacing it through the system. These pumps are known for their precise metering capabilities and ability to maintain a consistent flow rate. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in ensuring efficient cooling water circulation in various industrial applications.</p></p>
