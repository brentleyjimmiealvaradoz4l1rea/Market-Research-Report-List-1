<p><ul><li>Main Rotor Blade</li><li>Tail Rotor Blade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Helicopter rotor blades can be classified into two main types: main rotor blades and tail rotor blades. Main rotor blades are attached to the main rotor assembly and are responsible for lifting the helicopter off the ground and providing propulsion. Tail rotor blades, on the other hand, are located at the tail of the helicopter and are essential for stabilizing the aircraft by countering the torque produced by the main rotor. Both types of rotor blades are crucial for the safe and efficient operation of a helicopter.</p></p>
