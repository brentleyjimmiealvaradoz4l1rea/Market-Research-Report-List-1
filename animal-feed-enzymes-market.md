<p><p>Animal feed enzymes market can be categorized into different types based on their functions. Proteases are enzymes that aid in protein breakdown, improving nutrient utilization. Other types of enzymes may include lipases, which help in fat digestion. Each type of enzyme plays a crucial role in enhancing the overall digestibility and nutrient absorption in animals.</p></p>
