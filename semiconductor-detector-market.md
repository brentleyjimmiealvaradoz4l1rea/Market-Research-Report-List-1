<p><p>Semiconductor detectors are used in various applications such as nuclear physics, medical imaging, and security screening. Three common types of semiconductor detectors include surface barrier detectors, lithium drifting detectors, and high purity germanium detectors. Surface barrier detectors offer high sensitivity and fast response time. Lithium drifting detectors provide improved energy resolution and charge collection efficiency. High purity germanium detectors are known for their superior spectroscopic performance and are widely used in high-resolution gamma-ray spectroscopy applications. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
