<p><p>Overhead conductors and OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) are types of cables used in electrical transmission. The market for OPGW can be categorized into Layer Stranding Structure OPGW and Central Tube Structure OPGW. Layer Stranding Structure OPGW consists of multiple layers of aluminum wire strands wrapped around a central stainless steel tube, while Central Tube Structure OPGW has optical fibers enclosed within a central aluminum or steel tube. Each market type offers unique advantages in terms of strength, flexibility, and optical fiber protection.</p></p>
