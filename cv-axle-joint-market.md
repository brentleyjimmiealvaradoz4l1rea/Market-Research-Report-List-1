<p><strong>The CV Axle Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outboard Joints</li><li>Inboard Joints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CV axle joint market consists of two main types - outboard joints and inboard joints. Outboard joints are located on the outer end of the axle assembly and connect to the wheel hub, allowing for smooth transmission of power while accommodating the suspension movement. Inboard joints, on the other hand, are situated closer to the transmission and differential, transferring power from the engine to the wheels efficiently. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of a vehicle's drivetrain system.</p></p>
