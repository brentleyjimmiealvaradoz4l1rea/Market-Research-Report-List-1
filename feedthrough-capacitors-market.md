<p><strong>The Feedthrough Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer Feedthrough Capacitors</li><li>Multilayer Feedthrough Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Feedthrough capacitors are classified into two main types based on their design and construction: Single Layer Feedthrough Capacitors and Multilayer Feedthrough Capacitors. Single Layer Feedthrough Capacitors consist of a single layer of ceramic material with electrodes on each side, providing simple filtering capabilities. On the other hand, Multilayer Feedthrough Capacitors are constructed with multiple layers of ceramic material interleaved with electrodes, offering higher capacitance values and better filtering performance. Both types play a crucial role in minimizing electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits.</p></p>
