<p><strong>The Sensor Development Boards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adapter Board</li><li>Demonstration Board</li><li>Evaluation Board</li><li>Add-On Board</li><li>Breakout Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sensor development boards are devices used for testing and prototyping sensors. Adapter boards allow sensors to be connected to different platforms. Demonstration boards showcase the functionality of a sensor. Evaluation boards provide a platform for testing different features of a sensor. Add-On boards enhance the capabilities of existing sensor boards. Breakout boards simplify the process of connecting sensors by breaking out individual pins. Each type of board serves a specific purpose in the sensor development process, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
