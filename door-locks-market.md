<p><p>The door locks market can be categorized into two main types: wet suit and dry suit. Wet suit locks are typically used in environments where they may be exposed to water or moisture, such as outdoor gates or swimming pool areas. These locks are designed to withstand wet conditions and prevent rusting or corrosion. On the other hand, dry suit locks are used in indoor settings and are primarily focused on providing security and convenience. They are more commonly found on residential and commercial doors where moisture is not a concern.</p></p>
