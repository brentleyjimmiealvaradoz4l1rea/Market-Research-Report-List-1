<p><p>Electric baseboard heaters come in two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed baseboard heaters are permanently installed in a room and are often wired directly into the electrical system of a building. Portable baseboard heaters, on the other hand, can be moved from room to room and simply plug into a standard electrical outlet. Both types of heaters provide efficient, radiant heat and can be controlled with a thermostat for customizable comfort. Fixed heaters offer a more permanent heating solution, while portable heaters provide flexibility and convenience.</p></p>
