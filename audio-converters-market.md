Digital Audio Converters receive digital audio signals and convert them into analog signals for output through speakers or headphones. On the other hand, Analog Audio Converters receive analog audio signals and convert them into digital signals for processing or storage. Both types of converters play a crucial role in audio processing and playback, catering to different needs and preferences of consumers in the audio industry.</p></p>
