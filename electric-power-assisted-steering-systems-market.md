<p><strong>The Electric Power Assisted Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPAS</li><li>P-EPAS</li><li>R-EPAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Assisted Steering Systems (EPAS) can be classified into three main types based on their structure and working mechanism: Column Electric Power Assisted Steering (C-EPAS), Pinion Electric Power Assisted Steering (P-EPAS), and Rack Electric Power Assisted Steering (R-EPAS). C-EPAS uses an electric motor mounted on the steering column, P-EPAS has the motor attached to the steering gear, and R-EPAS has the motor integrated into the rack. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of performance, cost, and efficiency in steering systems.</p></p>
