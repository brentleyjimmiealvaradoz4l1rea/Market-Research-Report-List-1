<p><p>The Smart Sorting System market includes two main types: Linear Sorting Systems and Loop Sorting Systems. Linear Sorting Systems involve products moving in a straight line on a conveyor belt, allowing for efficient and precise sorting. Loop Sorting Systems, on the other hand, involve products circulating on a looped conveyor belt, enabling continuous sorting and high throughput. Both systems are commonly used in industries such as logistics, e-commerce, and manufacturing to streamline processes, improve accuracy, and increase productivity.</p></p>
