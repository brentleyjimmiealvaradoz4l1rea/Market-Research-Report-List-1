<p><p>AGVs in the pharmaceutical market are categorized into three main types: Unit Load Type, Automated Forklift Type, and Tugger Type. Unit Load AGVs are designed to transport entire pallets or containers of pharmaceutical products within a warehouse or distribution center. Automated Forklift AGVs are equipped with forks to lift and move individual pallets or containers. Tugger AGVs are programmed to pull multiple carts or trailers filled with pharmaceutical products around a facility. Each type offers unique benefits and efficiency for pharmaceutical operations.</p></p>
