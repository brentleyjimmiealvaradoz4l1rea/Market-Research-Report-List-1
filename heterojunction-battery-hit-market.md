<p><p>Heterojunction Battery (HIT) technology is a type of solar cell that consists of different semiconductor materials, designed to increase efficiency and performance. There are three main types of HIT markets: SHJ (Silicon Heterojunction), HDT (Heterojunction with Thin-layer Design), and Others. SHJ HIT cells use a combination of silicon and thin-film materials, HDT cells use a thin-film layer on top of a silicon base, and the Others category includes various combinations of different materials for unique applications and performance enhancements.</p></p>
