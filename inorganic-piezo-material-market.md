<p><p>Inorganic Piezo Material Market Types include Piezoelectric Crystals and Piezoelectric Ceramics. Piezoelectric Crystals are natural materials that exhibit piezoelectric properties, such as quartz and Rochelle salt. These crystals are capable of generating an electric charge when mechanical stress is applied. Piezoelectric Ceramics, on the other hand, are man-made materials with similar properties to piezoelectric crystals. These ceramics are commonly used in various applications such as sensors, actuators, and transducers due to their excellent piezoelectric properties.</p></p>
