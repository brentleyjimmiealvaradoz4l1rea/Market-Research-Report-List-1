<p><strong>The Electric Razor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Foil</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric razor market consists of two main types: foil and rotary. Foil razors have a thin, perforated foil covering the blades, providing a close shave with less skin irritation. Rotary razors have three circular blades that move in a circular motion, adjusting to the contours of the face for a smooth shave. Foil razors are typically preferred by those with sensitive skin or who want a precise shave, while rotary razors are popular for their flexibility and ability to handle longer hair.</p></p>
