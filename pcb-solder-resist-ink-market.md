<p><p>PCB Solder Resist Ink is categorized into three main types based on their curing methods: Photoimageable SRI, UV Curable SRI, and Thermal Curable SRI. Photoimageable SRI is applied through a photolithographic process, UV Curable SRI cures under UV light, and Thermal Curable SRI hardens when exposed to heat. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, accuracy, and durability in the PCB manufacturing process. Customers can choose the most suitable type based on their specific requirements and production needs.</p></p>
