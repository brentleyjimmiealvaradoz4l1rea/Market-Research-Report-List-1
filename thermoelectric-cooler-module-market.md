<p><p>Thermoelectric cooler modules are categorized into two main types: single stage modules and multistage modules. Single stage modules are commonly used for cooling applications where a temperature difference needs to be maintained between the hot and cold sides. On the other hand, multistage modules are used for applications requiring a larger temperature difference and higher cooling capacity. Both types of modules are widely utilized in various industries such as automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics for thermal management purposes.</p></p>
