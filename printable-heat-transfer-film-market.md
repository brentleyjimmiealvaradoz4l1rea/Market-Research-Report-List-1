<p><p>Printable Heat Transfer Film is a versatile product that comes in two main types: Heat Transfer Vinyl and Heat Transfer Plastic Film. Heat Transfer Vinyl is made of vinyl material that is cut into designs and then heat pressed onto the fabric. Heat Transfer Plastic Film is made of thin plastic material that can be printed on directly and then heat pressed onto the fabric. Both types are commonly used for creating personalized graphics, logos, and designs on garments, bags, and other textile items.</p></p>
