<p><p>Intelligent exoskeletons are robotic devices designed to enhance the physical abilities of individuals. These devices are classified into three main types: lower body, upper body, and full body exoskeletons. Lower body exoskeletons are primarily used to assist and enhance the lower limbs, enabling individuals with lower body disabilities to walk or perform tasks with ease. Upper body exoskeletons focus on aiding the upper limbs, providing support and strength for individuals with upper body impairments. Full body exoskeletons encompass both lower and upper body support, providing comprehensive assistance for individuals with complete mobility challenges.</p></p>
