There are two main types of high molecular weight polyisobutylene on the market: Chlorinated PIB (C-PIB) and Hydroxyl-terminated PIB (HR-PIB). C-PIB is commonly used as a thickening agent in lubricants and greases, while HR-PIB is utilized in sealants, adhesives, and personal care products due to its excellent flexibility and moisture resistance.
