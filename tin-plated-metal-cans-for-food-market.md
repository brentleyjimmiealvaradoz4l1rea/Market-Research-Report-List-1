<p><p>Tin-plated metal cans for food come in two main market types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are made by welding together three separate components - the body, lid, and bottom. These cans are typically used for packaging products that require a strong and durable container, such as canned vegetables. Two-piece cans, on the other hand, are made from two pieces of metal that are formed and sealed together. These cans are commonly used for packaging beverages and canned fruits.</p></p>
