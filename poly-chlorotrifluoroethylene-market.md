In Suspension Polymerization, solid particles of PCTFE are dispersed in a liquid medium and polymerized to form the final product. On the other hand, Emulsion Polymerization involves dispersing PCTFE monomers in water with the help of surfactants to produce a stable emulsion that is polymerized to create the desired PCTFE product.</p></p>
