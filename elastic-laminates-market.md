<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Elastic Laminates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ethylene-based Elastomers</li><li>Propylene-based Elastomers</li><li>Styrene-block Copolymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elastic laminates market includes three main types of materials used for elasticity: Ethylene-based elastomers, Propylene-based elastomers, and Styrene-block copolymers. Ethylene-based elastomers, such as ethylene vinyl acetate (EVA), exhibit excellent flexibility and elasticity. Propylene-based elastomers, like polypropylene (PP), offer good strength and stretch properties. Styrene-block copolymers, such as styrene-butadiene-styrene (SBS), provide a balance of elasticity and durability. Each of these materials is widely used in various industries for manufacturing elastic laminates for products like diapers, medical dressings, and hygiene products.</p></p>
