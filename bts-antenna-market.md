<p><p>The BTS (Base Transceiver Station) antenna market consists of two main types: single-band and multiple-band BTS antennas. Single-band antennas are designed to operate on only one frequency band, providing excellent performance for a specific network technology. On the other hand, multiple-band antennas can operate on multiple frequency bands simultaneously, making them versatile and suitable for various network technologies. Both types cater to the specific needs of different network operators, with single-band antennas offering specialized performance and multiple-band antennas offering flexibility and compatibility with different technologies.</p></p>
