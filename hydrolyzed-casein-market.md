Instant Micellar Casein is a form of hydrolyzed casein that is quickly soluble in water, making it ideal for use in protein shakes or smoothies. Micellar Casein is a slower digesting form of protein derived from milk, providing a sustained release of amino acids to the body over a longer period of time.
