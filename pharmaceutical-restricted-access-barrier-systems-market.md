<p><p>Pharmaceutical Restricted Access Barrier Systems have two main types: Closed Type and Open Type markets. Closed Type systems provide a complete barrier between the operator and the product, ensuring maximum protection for both. On the other hand, Open Type systems allow for some interaction between the operator and the product, providing a higher level of convenience but with slightly lower levels of protection. Both types cater to the specific needs of pharmaceutical manufacturers in ensuring the integrity and safety of their products.</p></p>
