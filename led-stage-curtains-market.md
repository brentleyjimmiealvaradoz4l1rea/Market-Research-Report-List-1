<p><p>The LED Stage Curtains market can be divided into two main types: monolithic and stitching. Monolithic LED Curtains are made from a single seamless piece of fabric, providing a smooth and uniform display. On the other hand, stitching LED Curtains are made by stitching smaller pieces of fabric together, offering more flexibility in terms of design and installation options. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the entertainment industry, providing unique solutions for stage decoration and lighting effects.</p></p>
