Unidirectional converters only allow signal conversion from one voltage domain to another, whereas bidirectional converters allow conversion in both directions. Both types of level shifters are essential in various applications, such as interfacing between different components or systems that operate at different voltage levels to ensure proper communication and functionality.</p></p>
