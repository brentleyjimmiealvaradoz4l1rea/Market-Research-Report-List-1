<p><p>Automotive Gear Lubrication market consists of two main types: Mineral Lubricants and Synthetic Lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from natural crude oil and are known for their cost-effectiveness and widespread availability. On the other hand, synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered to provide superior performance in terms of temperature resistance, oxidation stability, and lubricity. They are often preferred for high-performance vehicles or extreme operating conditions. Both types of lubricants play a crucial role in ensuring smooth operation and longevity of automotive gear systems.</p></p>
