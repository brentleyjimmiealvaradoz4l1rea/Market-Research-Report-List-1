<p><p>Polyurea protective coating market can be divided into two main types: pure polyurea coating and hybrid polyurea coating. Pure polyurea coating is made entirely of polyurea, providing high levels of durability, chemical resistance, and abrasion protection. On the other hand, hybrid polyurea coating combines polyurea with other materials like polyurethane or epoxy to achieve specific performance characteristics such as UV resistance or flexibility. Both types offer excellent protective properties, but hybrid coatings can be customized to meet specific requirements.</p></p>
