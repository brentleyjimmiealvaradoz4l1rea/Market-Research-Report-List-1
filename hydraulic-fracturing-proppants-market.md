<p><ul><li>Frac Sand Proppant</li><li>Resin-coated Proppant</li><li>Ceramic Proppant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic fracturing proppants are used to prop open the fractures created in shale formations to allow for the extraction of oil and gas. There are three main types of proppants used in hydraulic fracturing: frac sand proppant, resin-coated proppant, and ceramic proppant. Frac sand proppant is made from high-purity silica sand, resin-coated proppant is coated with a resin to improve its strength and conductivity, and ceramic proppant is made from sintered bauxite or kaolin clay to withstand high pressures and temperatures.</p></p>
