<p><p>Large Format Displays are categorized into LED Backlit and CCFL markets based on the type of technology used to illuminate the display panel. LED Backlit displays use light-emitting diodes to provide vibrant colors, high contrast ratios, and energy efficiency. On the other hand, CCFL (Cold Cathode Fluorescent Lamp) displays use fluorescent tubes to generate light. LED Backlit displays are more commonly used due to their superior performance and longevity, while CCFL displays are being phased out due to their lower energy efficiency and shorter lifespan.</p></p>
