<p><strong>The Spectrum Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Tuned</li><li>Vector Signal</li><li>Real-Time</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swept-Tuned Spectrum Analyzers utilize a single frequency sweep to analyze signals. They are cost-effective and offer high dynamic range. Vector Signal Analyzers are advanced versions that provide in-depth analysis of complex modulated signals such as LTE and WLAN. They offer better accuracy and sensitivity compared to swept-tuned analyzers. Real-Time Spectrum Analyzers provide continuous monitoring of signals, capture transient events, and display results in real-time. They are ideal for monitoring dynamic and rapidly changing signals. Each type caters to specific needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
