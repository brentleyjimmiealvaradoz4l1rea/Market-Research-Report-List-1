<p><p>Copper Redistribution Layer (Cu RDL) and Copper/Nickel/Gold Redistribution Layer (Cu/Ni/Au RDL) are both types of metal layers used in advanced packaging technologies to redistribute electrical signals on semiconductor devices. Cu RDL is solely made of copper, while Cu/Ni/Au RDL includes copper, nickel, and gold layers. Cu RDL is more commonly used for its cost-effectiveness, while Cu/Ni/Au RDL offers better conductivity and corrosion resistance. These market types cater to different packaging requirements and are chosen based on specific performance criteria.</p></p>
