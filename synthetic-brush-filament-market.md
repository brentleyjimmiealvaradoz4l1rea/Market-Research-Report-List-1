<p><p>The synthetic brush filament market can be divided into four main types: Nylon, Polyester, PP (Polypropylene), and Other. Nylon filaments are durable and have good bend recovery, making them suited for a wide range of applications. Polyester filaments are known for their resistance to chemicals and high temperatures. PP filaments are lightweight and resistant to moisture, making them ideal for outdoor use. The "Other" category includes filaments made from materials like PBT (Polybutylene terephthalate) or PET (Polyethylene terephthalate), offering unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
