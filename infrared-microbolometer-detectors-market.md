<p><ul><li>Vanadium Oxide (VOx)</li><li>Amorphous Silicon (A-Si)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared microbolometer detectors are classified into types based on the material used in the detector array. The three main types are Vanadium Oxide (VOx), Amorphous Silicon (A-Si), and Others. VOx detectors offer high sensitivity and pixel uniformity, making them ideal for high-end applications. A-Si detectors are cost-effective and widely used in commercial applications. The 'Others' category includes detectors made from alternative materials such as MCT or superlattice, offering unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
