<p><p>Etching metal products can be categorized into two main market types: Wet etching and dry etching. Wet etching involves immersing the metal in a chemical solution to remove material, while dry etching involves using plasma or reactive gases to etch the metal surface. Wet etching is often preferred for its cost-effectiveness and simplicity, while dry etching offers greater precision and control over the etching process. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences in the metal etching industry.</p></p>
