<p><p>Aluminium oxide paper market types include the Slurry Method, which involves mixing aluminium oxide with a liquid and coating it on a backing material. The Dipping Method immerses the backing material in a solution of aluminium oxide to create the abrasive surface. The Sol-gel Method uses a sol-gel process to create a thin film of aluminium oxide on the backing material. Other methods in the market may include variations or combinations of these processes to produce different types of aluminium oxide paper.</p></p>
