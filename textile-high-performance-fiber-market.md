<p><p>Textile high-performance fibers can be classified into various types based on their unique properties and applications. Carbon fiber offers high strength and low weight, while aramid fibers provide excellent heat resistance. PBI fibers are known for their flame and chemical resistance, and PPS fibers offer superior chemical stability. Glass fibers are commonly used for their high electrical insulation properties, and high strength polyethylene fibers are valued for their exceptional durability. Other types of high-performance fibers include Nomex, Kevlar, and Vectran, each catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
