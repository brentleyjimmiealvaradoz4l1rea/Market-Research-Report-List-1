<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) is categorized into three main market types based on the vinyl acetate (VA) content. EVA with VA content up to 4% is mainly used for packaging and non-durable applications. EVA with VA content between 4 to 30% is used in flexible packaging, footwear, and solar cell encapsulation. EVA with VA content greater than 40% is typically used in high-performance applications such as photovoltaic encapsulation, hot melt adhesives, and impact modification in plastics.</p></p>
