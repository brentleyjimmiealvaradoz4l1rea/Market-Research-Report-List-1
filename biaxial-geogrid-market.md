<p><p>Biaxial geogrids are primarily categorized into three main types based on their manufacturing process: extruded, knitted/woven, and bonded. Extruded geogrids are made by extruding plastic materials to form a grid structure, while knitted/woven geogrids are created by weaving or knitting fibers together to create a grid pattern. Bonded geogrids are composed of materials that are bonded together to form a grid structure. Each type of geogrid offers specific characteristics and advantages for various applications in the construction and civil engineering industries.</p></p>
