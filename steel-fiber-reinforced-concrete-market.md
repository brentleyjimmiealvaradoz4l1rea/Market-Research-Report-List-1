<p><p>Steel Fiber Reinforced Concrete Market is divided into four main types based on the shape of the fibers: Cut Wire (Cold Drawn) Steel Fiber Type, Slit Sheet Steel Fiber Type, Melt Extract Steel Fiber Type, and Mill Cut Steel Fiber Type. Cut Wire (Cold Drawn) Steel Fiber Type is derived by cutting wire into desired lengths. Slit Sheet Steel Fiber Type is produced by slitting thin sheets of steel. Melt Extract Steel Fiber Type is created by melting steel and extracting fibers. Mill Cut Steel Fiber Type is produced by cutting solid bars into fibers.</p></p>
