<p><p>Digital Textile Printing Dyes are categorized into various types based on their properties and applications. Acid inks are used for printing on natural fibers like silk and wool. Disperse inks are ideal for synthetic fibers such as polyester. Dye-based inks are versatile and can be used for multiple fabric types. Reactive inks are best for printing on cotton and other cellulosic fibers. Sublimation inks are used for printing on polyester fabrics and provide vibrant colors and durability. Each type of ink caters to specific textile printing requirements.</p></p>
