The four main types of harbor fenders are solid rubber fenders, pneumatic fenders, foam fenders, and timber fenders. Solid rubber fenders are durable and cost-effective, while pneumatic fenders offer high energy absorption. Foam fenders are lightweight and easy to install, ideal for temporary applications.
