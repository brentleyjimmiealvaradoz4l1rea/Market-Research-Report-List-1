<p><strong>The OLED Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HTL/BTL/HBL</li><li>HIL</li><li>Encapsulation</li><li>EML</li><li>Substrate</li><li>Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OLED material market consists of various types including HTL/BTL/HBL, HIL, Encapsulation, EML, Substrate, and Anode markets. HTL/BTL/HBL materials are used as hole transport layers in OLED devices. HIL materials act as hole injection layers. Encapsulation materials protect OLED displays from moisture and oxygen. EML materials emit light in OLED devices. Substrates provide a base for OLED layers. Anode materials are used as electrodes in OLED devices to inject holes into the organic layers. Each type of material plays a crucial role in the functioning of OLED displays.</p></p>
