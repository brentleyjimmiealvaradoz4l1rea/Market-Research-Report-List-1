<p><p>U Profiled Glass comes in two main market types: Transparent and Translucent. Transparent U Profiled Glass allows for the passage of light without distorting images or colors, making it ideal for applications where clarity is key, such as windows or display cases. On the other hand, Translucent U Profiled Glass allows light to pass through while diffusing it, providing privacy and a soft glow. It is commonly used in applications where some level of privacy is desired, such as partition walls or shower doors.</p></p>
