<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sortation & conveyor systems are used in warehouses and distribution centers to efficiently sort and move products. Linear sortation systems involve products being moved along a straight line and diverted into different lanes based on predetermined criteria. Loop sortation systems use a continuous loop track where products are sorted and redirected using various methods such as diverters and gates. Both systems are essential for streamlining the fulfillment process, improving accuracy, and increasing productivity in industries such as e-commerce, retail, and manufacturing.</p></p>
