<p><p>Blister lidding foils are categorized into three main types based on their composition and intended use. Single layer foils, also known as push through foils, are commonly used for conventional blister packaging. 2-layer foils, such as paper/foil combinations, provide enhanced barrier properties and are suitable for products sensitive to moisture and light. Child-resistant foils feature special locking mechanisms to prevent easy access by children, making them ideal for packaging medications and other potentially harmful products. Each type of blister lidding foil serves specific packaging needs in various industries.</p></p>
