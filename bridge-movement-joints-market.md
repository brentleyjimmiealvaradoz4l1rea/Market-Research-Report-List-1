<p><p>Bridge movement joints are essential components in bridge construction that allow for the movement of the bridge deck due to factors like temperature changes, traffic loads, and seismic activity. There are two main types of bridge movement joints: open joints and closed joints. Open joints allow for visible movement of the bridge deck, usually covered with a waterproof material. Closed joints are designed to provide a seamless appearance while still accommodating movement of the bridge deck. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safety and longevity of bridges.</p></p>
