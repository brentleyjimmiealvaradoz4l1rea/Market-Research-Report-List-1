<p><p>Solid Microneedles, Hollow Microneedles, and Dissolvable Microneedles are types of technologies used in the field of trans/intradermal drug delivery. Solid Microneedles are tiny needles that painlessly penetrate the skin to deliver drugs, while Hollow Microneedles have channels through which drugs can be directly injected. Dissolvable Microneedles are made of biodegradable materials that dissolve after delivering the drug. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of drug delivery efficiency, ease of use, and patient comfort, making them valuable tools in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
