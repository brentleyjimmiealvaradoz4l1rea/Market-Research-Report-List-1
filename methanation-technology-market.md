<p><p>Methanation technology includes two main market types: one that converts carbon dioxide (CO2) into methane and another that converts carbon monoxide (CO) into methane. The process of converting CO2 into methane involves capturing CO2 emissions from sources like industrial processes or power plants and reacting them with hydrogen to produce methane. On the other hand, converting CO into methane typically involves using syngas derived from sources like biomass or natural gas to create methane through a catalytic reaction.</p></p>
