<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Wrap Film market offers two main types of films: Cast Film and Calendered Film. Cast Film is made through a casting process where liquid resin is poured onto a casting sheet, resulting in a film with superior conformability and durability. On the other hand, Calendered Film is formed by pressing a heated resin through a series of rollers, making it more cost-effective but less conformable than Cast Film. Both types of films are used in the automotive industry for vehicle wraps and customization.</p></p>
