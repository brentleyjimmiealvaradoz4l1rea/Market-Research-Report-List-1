<p><p>High Temperature Stainless Steel market can be categorized into two main types - High Temperature Austenitic Grades and High Temperature Ferritic Grades. High Temperature Austenitic Grades exhibit excellent toughness, weldability, and corrosion resistance at elevated temperatures, making them suitable for applications in industries like power generation and chemical processing. On the other hand, High Temperature Ferritic Grades are known for their oxidation resistance and thermal stability, making them ideal for applications where high temperatures are present, such as in automotive exhaust systems and industrial furnaces.</p></p>
