<p><p>Wood primers are essential for preparing wood surfaces for paint or stain. There are three main types of wood primers on the market: oil-based primer, water-based primer, and shellac-based primer. Oil-based primers are known for their durability and excellent adhesion to wood surfaces. Water-based primers are eco-friendly and offer quick drying times. Shellac-based primers are ideal for sealing in wood stains or tannins. Each type of primer has its own unique benefits and is suitable for different wood surfaces and applications.</p></p>
