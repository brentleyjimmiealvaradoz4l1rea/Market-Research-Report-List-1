<p><p>Hydrocarbon Ceramic Laminates are used in various industries and typically classified into three main types: Common FR-4, Mid-Tg FR-4, and High-Tg FR-4. Common FR-4 laminates are the most widely used and cost-effective option for general applications. Mid-Tg FR-4 laminates have a higher glass transition temperature, making them more suitable for applications requiring higher thermal stability. High-Tg FR-4 laminates have the highest glass transition temperature, making them ideal for high-temperature applications where thermal performance is critical.</p></p>
