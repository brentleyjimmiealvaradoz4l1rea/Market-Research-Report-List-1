<p><strong>The Automotive Dry Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Disc Dry Clutch</li><li>Multi-disc Dry Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Dry Clutch Market can be categorized into two main types: Single Disc Dry Clutch and Multi-disc Dry Clutch. </p><p>A Single Disc Dry Clutch is a type of clutch that consists of a single friction disc between the flywheel and pressure plate. It is commonly used in vehicles with lower torque requirements.</p><p>On the other hand, a Multi-disc Dry Clutch consists of multiple friction discs and is designed for vehicles with higher torque demands. This type of clutch provides better power transfer and durability for high-performance applications.</p></p>
