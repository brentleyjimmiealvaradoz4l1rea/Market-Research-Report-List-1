<p><p>Coated sand cores are used in the foundry industry for creating internal cavities in metal castings. There are two main types of coated sand core production methods: Cold Method and Thermal Method. In the Cold Method, the sand mixture is cured at room temperature using a binder, while in the Thermal Method, the sand is heated to cure the binder. Both methods offer advantages in terms of cost-effectiveness, production efficiency, and the ability to create complex shapes for a wide range of applications in the manufacturing sector.</p></p>
