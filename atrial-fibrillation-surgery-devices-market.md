<p><p>Atrial Fibrillation Surgery Devices market includes two types: Catheter Ablation and Maze Surgery. Catheter Ablation is a minimally invasive procedure where a thin tube (catheter) is inserted into the heart to destroy abnormal heart tissues causing irregular heartbeats. Maze Surgery is an open-heart surgical procedure that creates scar tissue in the atria to redirect the electrical signals and restore regular heart rhythm. Both techniques aim to treat patients with atrial fibrillation, a condition characterized by irregular and rapid heart rhythm.</p></p>
