<p><strong>The Distribution Automation Controllers (DAC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed Type</li><li>Centralized Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distribution Automation Controllers (DAC) can be categorized into two main types based on the control and management architecture: Distributed Type and Centralized Type. </p><p>In Distributed Type market, the controllers are distributed across various nodes within the distribution network, allowing for more localized control and decision-making. This type is preferred for systems with a high degree of autonomy and self-healing capabilities.</p><p>On the other hand, Centralized Type market involves a centralized control and management system that coordinates and supervises all controllers from a single location. This type is more suitable for networks with complex operations that require centralized monitoring and control.</p></p>
