<p><p>Engineered Quartz (E-Quartz) Countertops are available in two main market types: Phaneromer Quartz and Cryptocrystalline Quartz. Phaneromer Quartz is characterized by its visible crystal structure and is commonly used in high-end countertop designs. On the other hand, Cryptocrystalline Quartz features a more compact structure with crystals that are not easily visible, making it a popular choice for more understated countertop options. Both types offer durability, low maintenance, and a wide range of color and design options for consumers to choose from.</p></p>
