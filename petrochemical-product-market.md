<p><p>Petrochemical products can be categorized into two main types: low-carbon alkenes and aromatic hydrocarbons. Low-carbon alkenes are a key component in the production of plastics and synthetic rubbers due to their flexibility and strength. Aromatic hydrocarbons, on the other hand, are commonly used in the production of solvents, resins, and adhesives. Both markets play a crucial role in various industries, from construction to automotive, and are essential for the development of modern materials and products.</p></p>
